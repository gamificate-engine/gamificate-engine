What is GAMIFICATE?

GAMIFICATE is an open source gamification engine.
In other words, it's the tool that will help you introduce gamification in your application or environment!.
- "But why should I use your engine and not another one?", you ask.
Well, it's very simple. Our engine is:

  • 1. Easy to start
  • 2. Easy to setup
  • 3. Easy to use
      • And we also offer you a generous free plan, so you can start to GAMIFICATE anytime! We provide an API with which you can connect your application with your GAMIFICATE data.

Getting Started

Registration

Create a GAMIFICATE account and login

Create a Realm

A Realm is the application or environment where you want to implement the gamification mechanics. It will contain all the information and data regarding the gamification: the users, the badges, the rewards, the users' progress on the badges, which rewards have been redeemed by each user and which didn't, etc...

Add Users, Badges and Rewards

You must add them in order to have a functional Realm, or else it would be useless! You can add users through our app in your Realm's dashboard, through the upload of a JSON file or even through our API. However, badges and rewards can only be created within our web app. But you can still get their information using our API.

Implement your Gamificate Realm in your own application

Now that you have everything set up, you only need to call our API to interact with your gamification data! You only need to send requests to our API whenever you want to display the users' leaderboards, to redeem a given reward or add progress on a badge! We got your back on the rest: we will take care of the leveling system, we will take care of the badges' completion, informing you when a user completes a badge after you add progress on it, and we also take care of all the hosting! This means you don't need to worry with keeping your own database for gamification purposes and keep it running 24/7. We got your back there too.

About

We are a group of Software Engineering students from Universidade do Minho, Portugal that love videogames and everything related to it. GAMIFICATE came up as a project for one of our MSc course unit. Given the lack of gamification tools like this one, we decided to embrace the project and provide the world an easy way of adhering to the wonderful world of videogames. With it, we expect to add competitivity into every environment possible, allowing its users to surpass themselves and achieve better results in real life.
